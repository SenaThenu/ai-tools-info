Looka

Looka is an AI-based logo-generating model.

Capabilities

  • Setting up brand kits and always depicting the user's brand identity.
  • Generating logos that suit the requirements of the user.
  • Generating more and more logos as the user scrolls down.
  • Presenting how logos will look on different surfaces.

How You Can Use This

As an Entrepreneur or a Student

Nowadays, it costs a considerable amount of money to create a custom logo. So, without wasting time trying to come up with ideas and struggling to learn a logo designing tool, here is a golden opportunity to both have inspiration and download final logo designs by spending only a few seconds. These logos can be used for your business, assignments or pretty much anywhere you can think of, as you gain the full rights for them.

Pricing

Even though this tool is available to be tested online, it costs some money. You can find different plans and an FAQ section by following this link. Either way, yet, since it provides you with a wide variety to select from, it is worth when compared with the money you pay for freelance logo designers just for one logo. Not to mention, even the time you waste...
