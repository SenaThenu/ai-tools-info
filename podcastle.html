Podcastle

Podcastle is an audio-creating AI tool. It can turn mere text into studio-quality audio clips! In addition, it provides many features to enhance any audio file (yep, even those recorded by you).

Capabilities

  • Converting text to speech.
  • Providing different tracks for the user to combine into one perfect audio clip.
  • Editing and enhancing audio clips. (Such as auto-levelling and dynamic fading)
  • Creating a digital copy of your voice that speaks the words you type!

How You Can Use This

As a Student or an Entrepreneur

Let's be frank! The chances are, you might have an extremely hilarious voice which sounds more hilarious when recorded or you might be super-shy to speak into a microphone and listen to what you just said. No matter what, here's an awesome lifesaver. For anything that requires explaining, type what you want to say and boom! You'll be given exactly what you need in the highest quality!

Pricing

Even though you can try most of Podcastle's features for free, if you want to increase the quality of your audio clips, you might need to buy a paid plan. You can find all the information you need regarding that over here.
