Chat-GPT

Chat-GPT is a generative AI created by Open AI. This uses the GPT language model to understand human language and generates highly intelligent responses based on deep neural networks.

Capabilities

  • Supporting more than 95 different natural languages.
  • Generating texts (essays, reports, reviews, blog posts, poems and even songs) in the required style and tone.
  • Summarising texts effectively.
  • Providing information concisely.
  • Coding in a wide range of programming languages.
  • Identifying bugs in scripts.

How You Can Use This

As an Entrepreneur

You can use Chat-GPT as a personal assistant to accomplish repetitive tasks. Or even as a chatbot to interact with your customers on your website or any social media platform. Additionally, this can help you analyse customer data and identify patterns and trends. Overall, with Chat-GPT handling these tasks, you can reduce the cost and provide your products at a lower price, keeping your customers happy!

As a Student

Chat-GPT can be a study buddy to erase boredom when studying. Why not? You can even tell him secrets and ask for suggestions. Plus, as a study buddy does, he can help you with your assignments and research. Moreover, Chat-GPT can be the best place for you to acquire inspiration while brainstorming new ideas.
